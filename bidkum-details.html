e-Bidkum Jabar

Project information

  • Category: API for mobile app
  • Client: Polda Jabar
  • Project date: 12 April, 2021
  • Project URL: e-Bidkum Jabar on Google Play

In this project, we collaborate with Bidkum Polda Jabar to create an e-Bidkum Jabar application that is used to help people who need information about the legal field of the regional police, especially in the West Java area. Users can also conduct legal consultations, and chat directly with operators from the legal field of the West Java Regional Police.
